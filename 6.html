Archaeological Record of Ogua

There is evidence to support the claim that certain fossils exhibited at the Carnegie Natural History Museum are in fact fossils of Ogua. This evidence is based on a variety of factors, including the physical characteristics of the fossils themselves, as well as their geographic location and the geological history of the area in which they were discovered.
